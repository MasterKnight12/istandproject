5 Lessons Learned the Hard Way

01

Don't Preparing your Schedule

Leave time for hobbies. Burnout is real.

02

Stop Procrastinating

You will miss a lot of good opportunities around your life.

03

Create a 4-Year Plan Early

Know what classes to take. Don't just go through semesters blindly.

04

Ask for Advice

Advisors, professors, and older students are there to help. Don't guess.

!!

If you get a Scooter or Bike, Buy an U-Lock Immediately

Crucial: Basic locks are easy to break. I learned this after my electric scooter was stolen.
